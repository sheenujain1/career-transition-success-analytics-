# career-transition-success-analytics-
This project analyzes workforce and career transition trends using Python and Power BI. The goal was to identify the factors that most influence successful career switching and transition readiness across different occupations and education levels.

The dashboard provides interactive insights into:

1) Career transition success probability
2) Skills gap analysis
3) Technology adoption
4) Professional networking impact
5) Certification effectiveness
6) Occupation-wise transition readiness

Business Problem

Career transitions have become increasingly common in the modern workforce due to:

1) technological disruption,
2) evolving job markets,
3) automation,
4) changing skill demands.

This project aims to answer:

1) What factors increase successful career transitions?
2) Which occupations are most transition-ready?
3) How do certifications, networking, and mentorship affect career mobility?
4) Does higher education always improve transition success?

Tools & Techniques Used: Python, Power BI

Key Insights
1) Certifications are the strongest predictor of successful career transitions.
2) Strong professional networks significantly improve career mobility.
3) Lower skills gaps strongly increase transition readiness.
4) Technology adoption positively impacts adaptability.
5) Some occupations (Economists, Lawyers) demonstrate higher transition flexibility.
6) Advanced degrees alone do not guarantee successful career switching.
